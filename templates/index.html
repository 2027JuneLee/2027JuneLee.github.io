About Us!

MathDoneMad is a special project created by June (Junsoo) created within a 2-week time limit. It aims to give users an AI learning guide (fully integrated ChatGPT), curated resources, and a simple UI to help students survive AP Calculus and the SAT's math section! This is intended to be used to learn concepts, catch weak areas, and get unlimited practice questions for free and with ease. Unlike other websites, this is completely free and has minimal interruptions.

Features

Learn With AI

Submit images, texts, and questions for AP Calc and SAT math. AI will help you learn and give you example problems!

Math Survival Kit

Get access to a handmade SAT formula sheet and curated resources for AP Calc (made by June, who survived AP Calc)

Score Booster (AI)

Get points for solving/asking questions, solve problems with GPT, learn your weak areas, and watch your scores grow!

How to Use MathDoneMad

Sign Up or Log In (optional)

If you want to save all of your previous chatlogs, then sign up! If you change your browser or device, they will be gone.

Ask AI!

Upload text, PDF, and even image files! AI will help solve the questions there and explain it. Alternatively, ask AI to create a mock test, quizzes, or personalized lessons.

Check Your Profile

Check out what topics you asked GPT (to find your weak areas), total problems solved correctly, and much more to improve!
